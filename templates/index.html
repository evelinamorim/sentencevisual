The Time Annotation Inspector

Instructions

1) Each time expression is shown inside a blue box, and their order is the same as they appear in the text. The first is shown first, etc.

2) Also, time expression has the color (blue), except for Duration and Set type, in which case it presents the color (purple).

3) By hovering over an event, it is possible to see its attributes.

4) Events with a darker dashed border are of type Intensional Action or Intensional State, i.e., they are events that may or may not happen.

5) To highlight a sentence associated with a time expression, click on the time expression. To remove the highlight, click on the same time expression again. If you click on a different time expression, the highlight from the previously selected sentence will be removed, and the new sentence will be highlighted instead.
